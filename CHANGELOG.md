## [1.0.0] - 2024-01-XX

### Added
- Initial release of Sample Company Profile website
- Complete bilingual support (Indonesian & English) with language toggle
- Responsive navigation bar with dropdown menus
- Home page with hero section, company overview, services, and featured projects
- About pages (Company Profile, Vision & Mission, Our Team)
- Services pages (Overview, Product Development, IT Consulting, Marketing Solutions)
- Projects portfolio page with grid layout
- Careers page with job listings and application form
- Contact page with form validation and company information
- Footer component with company info, quick links, and contact details
- Sample notice modal on first visit
- Custom SVG logo and favicon
- Smooth scroll navigation
- Fade-in on scroll animations
- Server-side rendering (SSR) support
- TypeScript configuration
- Tailwind CSS styling
- Docker deployment configuration
